Teaching Approach

How I Teach —
and Why It Works

Not just grammar exercises. Real English, real conversations, real results.

01

We Start With You

Before I teach a single word, I listen. I understand your level, your goals and the specific gaps holding you back. Then I build your plan around exactly that.

02

Grammar With Purpose

Grammar is the skeleton of a language — but dry rules don't stick. I teach every grammar point inside real sentences and situations you will actually use.

03

Speaking from Day One

Many students wait until they are "ready" to speak. In my lessons you speak from the very first day — in a safe, encouraging space where mistakes are just steps forward.

04

Real English, Not Textbook English

I lived in London. I know the difference between what a textbook says and how English is actually spoken. My lessons give you both — so you are never lost in the real world.
